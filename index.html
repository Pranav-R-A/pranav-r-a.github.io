Tau Beta Pi stands as the nation's oldest engineering society and the second oldest collegiate honor society. Our chapter prioritizes cultivating comprehensive skills, equipping members for their professional journeys, and contributing to the community.

BENEFITS OF JOINING

TBP provides students with many career long benefits such as: an Online Job Board, TBP Recruitment Fair access, a gifted hire invitation, United Planet scholarship access, 20% off FE/EIT exams, and many other exclusive discounts (i.e. Gleen, Dell, 100+ hotels worldwide).
