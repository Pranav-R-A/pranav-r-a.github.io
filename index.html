INTEGRITY AND EXCELLENCE IN ENGINEERING

Tau Beta Pi honors students who demonstrate outstanding academic achievement, ethical behavior, and a commitment to serving the community. We celebrate leadership, service, and excellence in engineering.

BENEFITS OF JOINING

TBP provides students with many unique benefits, such as lifetime membership, scholarships, job opportunities, and a strong network of engineers. Members gain access to industry events, alumni mentorship, and leadership opportunities.
